Comprehensive Load Testing Solutions

With state-of-the-art equipment and certified engineers, we provide accurate and reliable load testing services that ensure your foundation meets all safety requirements and design specifications.

Static Load Testing

Comprehensive axial and lateral load testing to determine ultimate bearing capacity and settlement characteristics of piles and foundations.

Dynamic Load Testing

High-strain dynamic testing using PDA (Pile Driving Analyzer) to assess pile capacity and integrity during or after installation.

Pile Integrity Testing

Non-destructive testing using sonic echo and cross-hole sonic logging to detect defects and verify pile continuity.

Lateral Load Testing

Specialized testing to determine lateral capacity and deflection characteristics for structures subject to horizontal forces.
