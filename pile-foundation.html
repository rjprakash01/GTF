Pile Foundation Services

At GEE Tech Foundation, we specialize in providing comprehensive pile foundation solutions that ensure the structural integrity and longevity of your construction projects. Our advanced piling techniques are designed to handle various soil conditions and load requirements.

Our Pile Foundation Expertise

With over 20 years of experience in the foundation industry, we have successfully completed numerous pile foundation projects across various sectors including residential, commercial, and industrial developments.

Bored Cast-in-Situ Piles

High-capacity piles suitable for heavy loads and challenging soil conditions. Our advanced boring equipment ensures precise installation with minimal vibration.

Precast Driven Piles

Cost-effective solution for projects requiring quick installation. Our precast piles are manufactured to the highest quality standards.

Micro Piling

Ideal for restricted access areas and underpinning existing structures. Our micro piles provide excellent load-bearing capacity in limited spaces.

Load Testing

Comprehensive testing services to verify pile capacity and ensure compliance with design specifications and safety standards.
